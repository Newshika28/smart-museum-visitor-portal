Government Museum Chennai

Established in 1851, the Government Museum Chennai is one of India's oldest museums and a landmark institution for art, archaeology, anthropology, zoology and history.

The museum attracts thousands of visitors every year and showcases priceless collections that tell the story of India's rich civilization and cultural legacy.

Through our online booking platform, visitors can reserve tickets, manage bookings and access QR-based entry verification for a seamless experience.
